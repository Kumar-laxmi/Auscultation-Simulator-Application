- Runserver on Local-server:                    python3 manage.py runserver
- Runserver on Wireless LAN:                    python3 manage.py runserver 192.168.100.35:8000 
- ipAddress of Wifi:                            ipconfig getifaddr en0
- to execute:                                   python3 manage.py runserver <ip_addr>:<port_no>
- to clear table in SQLite:                     <model_name>.objects.all().delete()
